Penang, Malaysia has long be famous with its fame as “The Best Foodie Place” in Malaysia. Even with a lot of emerging food fusions of western cuisine and other Asian country specialties, Penang is still well known of its local conventional food choice. LKL Minimart is a grocery store that emplasized at the food quantility and operate more than 80 years in Penang, Malaysia. Fresh and the finest ingredients can be found for local homes and skillful chefs at local restaurants.
